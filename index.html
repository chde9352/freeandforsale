HOW IT WORKS?

It’s simple and smooth

Swipe Left to Buy

Everyone's doing it, gestures to navigate. We're trying to make this switch REAL easy. Just like tinder taught you.

When you open the app you will be presented with a choice, to swipe left to buy or swipe right to sell.

Swipe Right to Sell

Everyone's doing it, gestures to navigate. We're trying to make this switch REAL easy. Just like tinder taught you.

When you open the app you will be presented with a choice, to swipe left to buy or swipe right to sell.

Geo-Location Posting

We connect you with your local community to allow you to find people with the same buying and selling needs as you! Get your shiny used bike noticed by that guy down the street looking to make an addition to his sweet bicycle collection.

Photo & Video Posts

Photos are old news, to step things up another notch we’re introducing video. Now you will be able to take a video and move around your goods to in order to provide a better feel for the item you’re posting. Less questions- time saved, money earned.

Mobile Transactions

No cash. No problem. We've built mobile transactions into our app to make the process of buying and selling even easier. Forget taking the trip to the ATM, we are giving you the option to exchange payments right from your phone.

THE BRAND.

Community.

Free & For Sale is a community. We are focused on creating a world-class group of local buyers and sellers. F&FS helps you find those hidden treasures in your neighborhood. It's simple.. everything is better together.

Simplicity.

We want the experience of Free & For Sale to be a simple, elegant, and intuitive. If it over complicates the process, we don't want any part of it in our app. We want buying and selling to be simple, so we've committed ourselves to focusing on just that.

Value.

Our success and value is derived from the positive use of our app within your community. If it isn't making your life easier, then we are not building value. We're focused on the user, the way it should be.
